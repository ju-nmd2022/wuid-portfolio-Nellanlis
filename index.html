About Me, Myself and I

Usually one would start their personal introduction off by saying "Hello, my name is Nelly and I am 20 years old". That is exactly what I am going to do as well.

Hi! My name is Nelly and I am indeed 20 years old!

Nowadays I am situated in Jönköping to study New Media Design at Jönköping University. I grew up in a small town about one and a half hour from here with my family of 6 members. I am the oldest out of four sibilings, I have two younger sisters and one younger brother who just turned 7 years old. He constantly wanted to play Minecraft with me when I still lived with my parents.


In my freetime I play floorball, which is a very popular sport in Sweden. It is similar to ice hockey but it is not played on ice and there is a plastic ball instead of a puck. I have been participating in the sport since I was 6 years old and when I moved to Jönköping, I had to switch teams and was able to meet new teammates and friends. Most of the people I hang out with are friends that I met through playing floorball. Me and my friends have spread out to live in many different parts of Sweden, but we try to see each other as often as possible.

For a while, I also nisched myself into a corner of painting furniture, not just in one color, but also having smaller details on the piece of furniture. Almost like it was its own painting.
